This is Money.io

A Money denomination archive.

  • Learn More

Main idea

Scientific interest

It helps to know the history of bills and coins that have been circulating throughout history in different countries.

Citizen science

The community helps keep a record of these bills and coins, which can be related to events of the time that may be important or even the reason for the design they have at the time.

Information flow

Registered users can upload images of bills and coins they find, as well as ask for information about them or publish them if they know part of their history.

How it works

It's easy to contribute! Get your first submition in these four steps.

Create an account

Create an account with your email, username and nationality, and set your password. We will send you an email to confirm your account.

Take a photo of a coin or bill

Take a photo of only one coin or bill, with good illumination, position and angle, this is necessary in order to mantain a good quality dataset.

Upload your photo

Start a submission, it does not matter if it's already been submited, the more samples we have, the better.

Answer some questions

We need to classify our dataset, so we need to label it, we'll ask you about the origin, denomination, year, and description.
